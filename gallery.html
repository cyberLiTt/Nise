About Us

Our schools are committed to by heritage to the educational mission of the catholic church through the philosophy and spirituality of Cornelia Connelly, which is based on trust and reverence for the dignity of every person. We are committed to the Holistic development of each child and to maintaining a high sense of religious and academic excellence, community and family spirit which is prevalent in all our schools. Our philosophy of education challenges each child to reach his or her potential.
